The Best Movies According to Urvino

My top 3 movies of all time

Boys in the Hood

About the life of 3 young black men, living in their neighboorhood. All 3 with different paths in life.

Saving private Ryan

Trying to save and bring back a soldier whose only family member left after the rest of his brothers are killed in action.

Predator

About a team of soldiers who go into a jungle for a mission and end up going up against a creature they found while there.
